Write a program that solves two tasks and prints the answers to the screen — each on its own line.

1. A painter wants to paint 8 sections of a fence, each in one of 2 colors. How many unique color combinations are there? Use the exponentiation operator `**`.
2. You and two friends (3 people in total) bought 9 pastries and want to share them equally. How many does each person get? Use the division operator `/`.
